Important Reminders

  • Color Palette: We kindly request our guests to avoid wearing white, as it is reserved for the bride.
  • Footwear: Our reception will be held in a garden setting. We recommend comfortable footwear or avoiding thin stilettos to ensure you can enjoy the evening.
  • Weather: The ceremony is indoors, but the reception is in an open-air garden. You might want to bring a light shawl or jacket just in case it gets breezy.
