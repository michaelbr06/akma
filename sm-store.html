How to Gift

Option 1: Shop Online (Fast & Easy)

  1. Visit our SM Registry Link. Please note that you’ll need to Create/Sign-in your account with SM Store Gift Registry for this option.
  2. Choose a gift: Browse the list, select the item(s) you’d like to give, and add them to your cart.
  3. Check out: Follow the prompts to pay securely online.

Option 2: Shop In-Store (At Any SM Store)

  1. Visit the Registry Counter: Head to the Customer Service or Gift Registry desk at any SM Department Store nationwide.
  2. Present the event code below.
  3. Shop & Scan: Pick up the items from the department store shelves. When you pay at the cashier, make sure they scan the registry barcode so the item gets marked as "purchased" on our list.
